Export Configuration

To help the support team properly diagnose your problem, it helps to provide the team with your configuration settings. To export your configuration, complete these steps:

  1. Open your project in Velocity Studio and click Runtime > Run from the menu bar.
  2. In your Web browser, log on to the Configuration application by entering http://localhost:8080/cwf/config/index.html or http://localhost:8080/cwf/config as the URL.
  3. Enter upadmin as both your Username and Password, and then press the Enter key.
  4. In the Configuration application, select the node that you want and click the Export button to export its configuration.


  5. The Export Passwords Confirmation dialog appears, prompting whether you want to export passwords. If you want to include your passwords as part of the export, click the Yes button. Otherwise, click the No button to continue.



  6. Your configuration details appear in XML in a dialog. Press the Ctrl + A keys to highlight all information in the dialog, and then press Ctrl + C to copy all details.



  7. In a text editor, such as Wordpad, open a new document, paste your configuration details, and then save the file.
  8. Send your file as an attachment in your e-mail to the support team.
