Contact Support

You can contact the support team either by e-mail or by telephone.

During Business Hours

The support team can be contacted in two ways during the business day:

  • Send an e-mail to cw.support.group@ericsson.com, which is sent to all members of the support team.
  • Call either 905-405-2188 or 1-877-405-2188 and ask for support. If you receive the Interactive Voice Response (IVR) system, select the option to leave a voice message and the on-call person will be paged.

During Off-Normal Hours

If the issue can wait until the next business day, you can do one of the following actions:

  • Send an e-mail to the support email address (cw.support.group@ericsson.com)
  • Call either 905-405-2188 or 1-877-405-2188. You can either leave a voice message for a follow-up the next business day or leave a voice message for an immediate response. If an immediate response is required, the on-call person will be paged.
